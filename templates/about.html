About AI Stock Analyzer

Welcome to AI Stock Analyzer, your personal AI-powered assistant for investment research. Our mission is to democratize financial data analysis, making sophisticated stock insights accessible to everyone, from seasoned investors to curious beginners.

We leverage cutting-edge large language models and real-time financial data from trusted sources to provide you with concise, actionable, and easy-to-understand stock analysis. Instead of spending hours sifting through charts and reports, you can get a comprehensive summary and a clear recommendation in seconds.

Our Technology

Our platform is built on a robust Python backend using the Flask framework. For AI-driven insights, we integrate with Cohere's powerful Command R+ model, and for financial data, we rely on the comprehensive APIs provided by Financial Modeling Prep.
